Refund Policy

ChartXpert.io

1. Free Trial

We offer a 7-day free trial period for all new users. During this period, you can use the platform with full access to all features. If you decide to continue using the platform after the trial period, you must subscribe to one of our packages.

2. Subscription and Auto-Renewal

After the 7-day free trial period, you will need to choose a subscription package. Your subscription will renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless you cancel auto-renewal. You can cancel auto-renewal at any time in your account settings.

3. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your request must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date. Refunds will only be granted for the following reasons:

  • Technical issues preventing you from using the Platform.
  • Incorrect billing or duplicate charges.
  • Other exceptional circumstances, as determined on a case-by-case basis.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team at Support@chartxpert.io with the following information:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Order number and date of purchase.
  • Reason for the refund request.

Our team will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days. If approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, including:

  • Subscriptions that have already been used.
  • One-time purchases of digital goods.
  • Promotional or discounted items.

6. Changes to this Return Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Return Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our Platform.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us at Support@chartxpert.io
